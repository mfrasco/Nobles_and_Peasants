welcome page:
    This page has a link to a page that explains the rules of the party.
    It also has a text entry box that allows people to define some game parameters.
    Then, you can click enter game.

main page.
    After clicking enter game, this is the page that you go to.
    Here are the ways to interact with this page
        Click on the nobles and peasants logo and return to this page.
        Click on the kingdom and go to view of the kingdom table.
        Click on the nobles and go to the view of the leaderboard table.

        Sign into the party.
        Pledge allegiance to a noble.
        Buy a drink
        Kill a target
        Ban a peasant
        Make some money
