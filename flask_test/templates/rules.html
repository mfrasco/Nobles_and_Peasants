What is Nobles and Peasants?

Nobles and Peasants aims to provide a middle ground between a theme party and a regular party. For those who just want to drink and hang out with friends, you can do that without much interaction with the rest of the game. For those who want to embrace the inner-workings of this medieval society, you can do that too. Nobles and Peasants establishes the minimal amount of rules so that youre night can become as wild as your own imagination.

How Does Nobles and Peasants Work?

Each person at the party is assigned to be a noble or a peasant. You can make this assignment random, or you can control the status of the person that you want to be. Your status can change throughout the game.

At the start of the game, nobles are given coin and peasants are given nothing. Coin can be used to purchase alcohol from the Pope (a.k.a. the bartender). In order for a peasant to get alcohol, they must pledge allegiance to a noble, which allows the peasant to get drinks using that noble's coin. The noble with the most peasants in his or her army is the Almighty Ruler of Heaven and Earth. This position is highly desirable.
